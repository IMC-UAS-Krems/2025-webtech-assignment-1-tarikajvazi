About


Learn about me

I am Tarik Ajvazi. From a young age I was interested in programming and creating something out of an idea.

When I was 13, I started going to a programming school learning many different languages and technologies. Soon after, I started competing and earning prizes in my field, which was mostly web development and later implementing that to business solutions.

At 16, I landed my first client and made a simple landing page. As time went on, I started getting more and more complicated projects that really helped me learn the skills. Apart from that, I also created projects of my own like a Hotel Management System, which I successfully managed to get two clients in another country at the age of 17. At that time I also started working on creating a Digital Catalog Generator for a distribution company to make their work process a lot easier, and I still manage it to date. My last project of this kind was a Digital Wedding Album, an interactive platform for guests in a wedding.

Of course, I do not only do web development in my life, I also like math and I was third nationally last year, got one first place and two third places in my city. And outside of academics I enjoy martial arts.
